# Legal document templates

These files are **draft templates only**. They are **not legal advice**. Heart & Hustle (or your operating entity) should have **qualified counsel** review and adapt them for your jurisdiction, tax posture, Stripe setup (e.g. Connect vs manual payouts), and insurance.

Suggested workflow:

1. Counsel finalizes the **School Fundraising Agreement** (master).
2. For each approved school/campaign, complete a **Fundraiser Campaign Addendum**, sign it, and store the executed PDF (and W-9) in SuperAdmin records.
