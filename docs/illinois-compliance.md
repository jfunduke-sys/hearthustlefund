# Illinois fundraising compliance (product note)

Heart & Hustle Fundraising is designed around a **SuperAdmin approval gate**:

1. **School requests** capture administrator intent and contact information.
2. **Paperwork** is tracked with `paperwork_sent` / status transitions outside or alongside the app (email/mail).
3. **Fundraiser codes** are only generated after a request is **approved**, so coaches cannot launch a campaign until your process is satisfied.

This workflow supports Illinois school fundraising rules that require documentation before solicitation begins. **This document is not legal advice**—have counsel review your forms and process.
