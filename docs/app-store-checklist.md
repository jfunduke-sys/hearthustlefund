# App Store checklist — Heart & Hustle Fundraising

**First time launching?** Follow the ordered guide: **[MOBILE_STORE_LAUNCH_WALKTHROUGH.md](./MOBILE_STORE_LAUNCH_WALKTHROUGH.md)** (step-by-step from Expo login through both stores).

## App icons

- **iOS**: App Store requires 1024×1024 marketing icon; Xcode asset catalog sizes (20pt–1024pt @2x/@3x as applicable).
- **Android**: Adaptive launcher icons (foreground + background), legacy mipmap densities (mdpi–xxxhdpi).

## Screenshots

- **iPhone 6.7"** (1290×2796 or equivalent) — required for primary listing.
- **iPhone 5.5"** — often required for older device class.
- **iPad** — if supporting tablet; capture 12.9" where applicable.

## App Store description (template)

**Heart & Hustle Fundraising** helps student-athletes raise money for high school sports teams. Join your team’s campaign, text friends and family with your personal secure link, track donations, and send reminders—while your school keeps the majority of every gift.

## Privacy policy URL

Host the live privacy page (e.g. `https://your-domain.com/privacy`) before submission.

## Age rating

Target **4+** (no objectionable content). Disclose data collection (contacts for optional SMS, account email).

## Stripe

Enable automatic email receipts in Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Emails if you promise tax/charitable receipts via email.
